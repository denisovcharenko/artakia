About

The intersection is the point.

Most of what's needed to succeed already exists. The architecture is what's missing.

Artakia was built on a simple belief: great artists deserve great support. We combine the reach of a major-distribution network with the intimacy and vision of an independent label.

Beyond music, we've evolved into a full creative collective spanning entertainment, studio, digital strategy, and creative production for artists, labels, and cultural brands ready to go global.

Background

Where the work comes from.

( layers of experience )

Artakia draws on time spent across four disciplines. We have worked in talent management, representing leading screen talent and understanding how reputation is built and protected. In media management, positioning talent and projects across press and public.

In social media marketing and audience building, building presence that holds over time. And in video production across every phase, from concept to final cut. Together these experiences gave us one place where talent management, story, strategy and production work as one.
